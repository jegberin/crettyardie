Page copy
H1: Crettyard Businesses
Crettyard and its surrounding townlands support a varied local economy shaped by farming, practical services, skilled trades, specialist manufacturing, and small independent enterprise. The area is not defined by one single main street alone; it works more as a rural network of junction services, workshops, suppliers, growers, contractors and specialist businesses serving local people and customers further afield.

Introduction
This page brings together local businesses based in Crettyard and the immediate area, including retail, fuel, agricultural supply, building trades, engineering, stone, digital services, gardening, heritage and specialist support services. It is designed to help residents, visitors, and people with family or business links to the area find trusted local names in one place.

Supporting local enterprise
One of Crettyard’s strengths is the range of enterprise packed into a small rural area. Alongside practical day-to-day services, the locality also includes businesses in wildflower production, agricultural engineering, stone supply, machinery, retrofit, web services and heritage tourism, which gives the area a broader economic story than many people might expect.
